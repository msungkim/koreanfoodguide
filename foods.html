Popular Everyday Dishes

You can find these 6 dishes pretty much everywhere They give a simple taste of what Korean food feels like in daily life.

Kimchi

Fermented cabbage

Fermented with red pepper powder, garlic, and ginger, and is rich in lactobacillus and is good for health.

Bibimbap

Mixed rice bowl

Hot rice mixed with egg, vegetables, and gochujang. Everything is stirred together right before eating.

Tteokbokki

Street food snack

A sweet and spicy chewy rice cake, often found at street stalls with friends.

Korean Fried Chicken

Crispy shared dish

A crispy fried chicken with sweet and spicy sauce mixed with red pepper paste, soy sauce, garlic, and sugar.

Kimbap

Simple meal

It’s easy to carry, sliced into bite-sized pieces, and often eaten as a snack, lunch, or picnic meal.

Soybean Paste Stew

Daily soup

A savory Korean stew boiled with soybean paste, vegetables, and tofu, and has a light and deep taste.
