Popular Everyday Dishes

You can find these six dishes almost anywhere. They offer a simple taste of what Korean food feels like in daily life.

Kimchi

Fermented cabbage

Fermented with red pepper powder, garlic, and ginger. It is rich in lactobacillus and known for its health benefits.

Bibimbap

Mixed rice bowl

Hot rice mixed with egg, vegetables, and gochujang. Everything is stirred together right before eating.

Tteokbokki

Street food snack

Sweet and spicy chewy rice cakes, often enjoyed at street stalls with friends.

Korean Fried Chicken

Crispy shared dish

Crispy fried chicken coated in a sweet and spicy sauce made with gochujang, soy sauce, garlic, and sugar.

Kimbap

Simple meal

Easy to carry and sliced into bite-sized pieces. Often eaten as a snack, lunch, or picnic meal.

Soybean Paste Stew

Daily stew

A savory stew simmered with soybean paste, vegetables, and tofu, with a light but deeply flavorful taste.
